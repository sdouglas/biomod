BIOMOD is an annual competition for biomolecular design.

Undergraduate teams compete to see who can build the coolest stuff using the molecules of life. Past teams have used DNA, RNA, and proteins to build robots, computers, and prototypes for nano-therapeutics. Students lead projects each summer and then travel to Harvard in early November to present their work and win awards.
